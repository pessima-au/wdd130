Content

Home page

Why will people come to my site?
My portfolio website is a space where I will highlight my skills and provide a comprehensive overview of who I am as an aspiring professional web developer/programmer. It also serves as a platform to exhibit my work, qualifications, and achievements, making it a valuable resource for prospective employers and anyone interested in my skills and experiences. This will also serve as a reference for me to document and remember details of my past projects, and provide information for updating my resume and preparing for a job interview.

What questions will my visitors expect my site to answer for them?
Questions about me
Visitors to my site will expect to learn about me, including my background, education, work history, and personal information that helps them understand who I am as a professional. By visiting my site, people can have their questions about my skills and experiences answered as well and reach me if they have any further questions.
Skills and expertise
My portfolio will answer questions about my specific skills, areas of expertise, and the value I can bring to potential employers.
Portfolio showcase
Visitors will want to see samples of my work, projects I have completed and outcomes I have achieved to date.
Contact information
Visitors to my site will be provided with clear and easily accessible contact information for inquiries or job opportunities.

What types of activities will visitors want to complete on my site?
Explore my work
Visitors will browse through my portfolio, view my projects, and assess my abilities.
Learn about me
Visitors will read about my background, experiences, and any personal interests or values that are relevant to my professional persona.
Contact me
Visitors will have a clear contact detail that allows them to contact me easily.
Download my resume
Visitors who would like to review my resume will have access to download a copy from my personal site.
What information is necessary to complete any actions my site offers?
Portfolio content
I must ensure my portfolio is organized with detailed project descriptions, highlighting my skills and achievements. The goal is to create a positive first impression. This means that it has to be detailed and specific.
About me
I will need to write a compelling "About Me" section that provides an overview of my background and experiences.
Contact Information
I will also need to include a contact form or email address for inquiries, and links to my professional social media profiles.
Resume download
I must post a link to my resume and ensure it is up-to-date and well-formatted. I must ensure that the link works.

Why will my visitors come to my site instead of another to get information or complete the actions above?
Unique personal brand
My website represents my unique personal brand. It is tailored to highlight my individuality, skills, and achievements in a way that no other platform can replicate.
Comprehensive Information
My site offers a one-stop destination for all information about me, saving visitors time and effort compared to searching for scattered information across various sources. Visitors will find complete information all in one place that will not be available anywhere else. In addition, they have the opportunity to message me directly for any further questions or inquiries.
Visual Appeal
A well-designed and user-friendly portfolio website will engage visitors more effectively than a plain resume or scattered online profiles. My websiteâ€™s clean layout and consistent formatting will make it visually appealing and enhance user experience.
